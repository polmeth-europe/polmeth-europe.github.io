Postponement of the PolMeth Europe Meeting in Hamburg!

Some months ago we were very disappointed to be forced to announce the cancellation of the PolMeth Europe in March due to the starting Corona pandemic. We were hoping to be able to hold the conference in September instead. Facing the current situation, it will still be difficult in September 2020 to welcome all of you in Hamburg due to health and safety risks, travel registrations and conference regulations. We do not support the idea of a virtual conference. For us, the biggest aim and incentive of the first PolMeth Europe is to set up a more European based network of political methodologists. We would really appreciate to enable a get-together for starting a fruitful networking base and do not support the idea, that a virtual PolMeth Europe would be an equal alternative to achieve this aim. Therefore, we decided to cancel the conference in September 2020 and to postpone the PolMeth Europe to March 2021 in form of a hybrid conference.

More details regarding date and organization will follow. Conference registrations already purchased for Polmeth Europe 2020 will be refunded within the next weeks. In case of any problems with the refunding, please send an email to (memberservices@cambridge.org)[mailto:memberservices@cambridge.org].

We have not taken this decision lightly and very much regret not to be able to welcome you this year in Hamburg. We are very much looking forward to welcoming you in 2021 instead.

The PolMeth Europe Organizing Committee.
